The C30 - 6 - 358 is a1.2 inch
tweeter with ultra hard
ceramic dome in a 58 mm body.

A proprietary clamping tech-
nique provides easy mounting
and adapting to individual front-
plates.

As the dome breakup is above
the audible range and well
damped, no dome cutouts are
required.

A unique FEA optimized under-
hung motor design with vented
aluminium voice coil former and
double neodymium magnet gua-
rantees low energy storage,
excellent heat transfer and high
excursion capability for low
power compression and ultra
low distortion around 0.07% @
2.83V and 0.5% max. @ 110dB.

The new designed soft fabric
surround centers the moving

parts with improved linearity.

We recommend an application
above 1800 Hz.
